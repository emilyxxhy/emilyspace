About Me

Hi! I’m Emily Huynh, a sophomore at DePauw University, majoring in Computer Science and Business Analytics. I’m passionate about uncovering insights hidden in data and turning them into decisions that create real impact.

I’m skilled in data analysis, business intelligence, and machine learning, using tools like Python, SQL, and Excel to explore complex datasets. As the Founder and President of the DePauw Data Science Club, I’ve learned how to combine analytical thinking with leadership and creativity.

Outside of academics, I love exploring astrology and studying world charts, which deepens my fascination with patterns and human behavior. I also love making coffee, which actually inspired my recent data project analyzing Starbucks drink nutrition.
