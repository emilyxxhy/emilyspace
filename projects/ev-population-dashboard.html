Solving the
"Charging Desert."

The visual analysis instantly revealed a critical policy failure: Zip Code X had the city's second-highest EV density but ranked last in public charging availability. This allowed the city planning department to re-allocate $2M in infrastructure funding to the precise underserved block, maximizing the utility of taxpayer investment.

Final Reflections.

Technical Lesson

For big data viz, the Data Model is the invisible foundation. Without rigorous query optimization and star-schema principles, the most beautiful map becomes unusable in a production environment.

Public Sector Strategy

Data in the public sector must be tangible. Connecting numeric records to physical neighborhoods turned an abstract report into a decisive planning tool for city officials.
